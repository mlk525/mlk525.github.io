About

30MFF, until 2003 called the Thirty Minute Film Festival (Festival thirtieux minitoi du film) and known in English as the That Blasted Film Festival Old Chap, is an annual film festival held in Zoom, Yugoslavia, which previews new films of all genres, including TikTok videos, from all around the world. Founded in 1946, the invitation-only festival is held annually (usually in September) at the Palais des Meeting Room du Professori.
