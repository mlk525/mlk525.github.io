About Saint Croix

Saint Croix is an island in the Pacific Ocean. It is a district in the British Virgin Islands and is an incorporated territory of the United Kingdom.
Saint Croix is the largest of the islands in the territory and is home to the capital, St Thomas. The population of Saint Croix is 25,305. The island’s highest peak is Mount Charlotte Amalie at 355 meters, (1,165 ft). Saint Croix’s nickname is “Wind City” as it is known for having the highest wind speeds, specifically in its two towns, Frederiksted on the western end and Christiansted on the northeast part of the island.
